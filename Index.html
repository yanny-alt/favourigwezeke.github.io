    About Favour Igwezeke

    A data analyst with a background in pharmacy. I'm currently pursuing a Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) degree at the University of Nigeria, set to graduate in 2026. My passion for data analytics was sparked by my love for mathematics and statistics and further fueled by an inspiring encounter with a fellow data scientist during my second year of pharmacy school.

    To build my skills, I've completed online courses in data analytics, including certifications from Coursera and DataCamp. I actively participate in hackathons and am a member of various data science communities. I am particularly excited about predictive analytics and its ability to forecast trends and behaviors, with a strong interest in applying machine learning to personalized medicine and healthcare optimization.

    Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy watching productivity and Notion videos, reading Wikipedia plots about my favorite movies, and imagining that I can play the guitar. My interest in science fiction fuels my imagination and creativity, which I believe is crucial in solving complex data problems. I also love exploring new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in data science.

    I am naturally curious and detail-oriented, traits that help me delve deep into data to uncover meaningful insights. My goal is to become a leading data scientist in the healthcare industry, contributing to groundbreaking projects that improve patient care.
